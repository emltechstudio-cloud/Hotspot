How it works

Four steps. Done.

From file to live link in under a minute. No technical setup needed.

01

Sign up free

Email and password. That's it. No card, no verification email, no setup.

02

Upload your HTML file

Pick your HTML file and choose how long the site should stay live — 24, 48 or 72 hours.

03

Share the link

Your site is live at hotspot.afric.site/s/yourname. Send it to your client.

04

Delete when done

Once you're paid, delete it. Or let it auto-expire. No cleanup, no billing surprises.
